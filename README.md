# HR Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI Dashboard developed to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, job satisfaction, salary trends, and education background within an organization.
The dashboard provides actionable HR insights through KPIs, trend analysis, and visualizations to support data-driven decision-making and improve employee retention strategies.

## Dashboard Features
- Total Employees Analysis
- Active Employees Tracking
- Employee Attrition Analysis
- Attrition Rate Monitoring
- Average Age Analysis
- Average Monthly Salary Analysis
- Gender-wise Employee Distribution
- Age Group Analysis
- Department-wise Attrition Analysis
- Job Role Attrition Analysis
- Education Field Attrition Analysis
- Job Satisfaction Rating Analysis
- Marital Status Analysis
- Travel Frequency Analysis
- Workforce Demographics Overview

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Total Employees: 1470
- Active Employees: 1233
- Total Attrition: 237
- Attrition Rate: 16.12%
- Average Employee Age: 37 Years
- Average Monthly Salary: 6503

## Dashboard Insights

### Employee Demographics- Majority of employees belong to the 25–44 age group.
- Male employees slightly outnumber female employees.
- Married employees represent a significant portion of the workforce.

### Attrition Analysis
- Sales Representatives show the highest attrition rate.
- Laboratory Technicians and Human Resources roles also experience notable attrition.
- Employees aged 28–35 show the highest attrition counts.

### Department Analysis
- Research & Development contributes the highest share of attrition.
- Sales department follows with a significant attrition percentage.
- HR department records the lowest attrition count.

### Education Field Analysis
- Life Sciences employees account for the highest attrition.
- Medical and Marketing backgrounds show moderate attrition levels.
- Human Resources education field has the lowest attrition.

### Job Satisfaction Analysis
- Most employees report satisfaction ratings between 3 and 4.
- Research Scientists and Sales Executives represent a large share of satisfied employees.

## Business Recommendations
- Focus retention efforts on Sales Representatives and Laboratory Technicians.
- Investigate causes of attrition within the R&D department.
- Improve employee engagement programs for employees aged 25–35.
- Strengthen career growth and development opportunities.
- Regularly monitor job satisfaction to reduce turnover risk.
